## Dataset Overview

- Raw McDonald’s Nutrition Dataset: Contains nutritional information such as calories, fat, sugar, protein, and sodium values of McDonald’s food items.

- Cleaned McDonald’s Dataset: Processed dataset after handling missing values and improving data consistency.

- Raw Customer Segmentation Dataset: Includes customer purchase behavior and demographic details used for customer analysis.

- Cleaned Customer Segmentation Dataset: Processed dataset after cleaning and preprocessing customer data.

- Raw NYC Airbnb Open Data: Contains Airbnb listing details such as room type, price, location, availability, and reviews.

- Cleaned Airbnb Dataset: Preprocessed Airbnb dataset after handling missing values, duplicates, outliers, and datatype inconsistencies.

- Raw Google Play Store Dataset: Includes app details such as category, rating, installs, reviews, size, and pricing information.

- Cleaned Google Play Store Dataset: Processed Play Store dataset after removing null values, duplicates, and cleaning columns.

- Raw Twitter Sentiment Dataset: Contains Twitter text data with positive, negative, and neutral sentiment labels.

- Cleaned Twitter Sentiment Dataset: Processed Twitter dataset after removing missing values, duplicate records, and improving data quality.
